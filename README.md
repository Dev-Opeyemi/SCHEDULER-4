# ⚡ PES — Personal Execution System

PES is a mobile-first web app designed to remove decision fatigue from your day. Instead of asking "what should I do next?", PES tells you — based on your real timetable, deadlines, and priorities. It's not a planner. It's an execution system.

---

## 💡 The Concept

Most productivity apps make you do too much thinking. PES flips that. You set up your schedule and study items once, and the app takes over — showing you exactly what to do right now, what's coming next, and what you're at risk of missing. The system enforces structure so you don't have to.

---

## ✅ Core Features

- **Live Dashboard** — shows your current task, time remaining, and what's up next. Always up to date.
- **Weekly Schedule** — your full timetable hardcoded in. Every lecture, devotion, study block, meal, and rest period accounted for.
- **Flexible Tasks** — add tasks with a duration and priority. The engine automatically fits them into free slots in your day.
- **Study Tracker** — track assignments, lecture reviews, and test prep across all your courses. Auto-sorted by urgency and difficulty — no manual ordering.
- **Today View** — your daily battlefield. Shows remaining schedule events + urgent study items in one place, with recommended time slots for each.
- **Override System** — try to skip a high-priority task and the system blocks you, asks for a reason, flags the skipped task as *At Risk*, and reminds you 20 minutes later.
- **Push Notifications** — alerts for task starts, upcoming events, overdue study items, and halfway reminders for long blocks.
- **PWA Support** — installable on mobile like a native app, works offline.

---

## ⚡ Advantages

- Zero frameworks — pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No build tools, no setup.
- Opens instantly from a single file.
- All data saved locally in the browser — no account, no backend.
- Designed mobile-first but works on desktop too.
- Notifications that actually push you to act, not just inform.
